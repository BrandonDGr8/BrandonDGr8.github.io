Deep Space


Real Life Efforts - In Game Benefits






Where exercise and fitness betters yourself and your game

Deep Space


is a game that uses real world exercise data to influence abilities in game.

The game itself is a bullet-hell type game where players control a spaceship to survive as long as possible from swarms of enemy ships and projectiles.

Exercise data is taken from Google Fit information of users gathered via wearables like smartwatches and fitness trackers
