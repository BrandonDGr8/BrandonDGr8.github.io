Instructions

Healing Humanity is a game focused on promoting world equality. You must keep the human body, or the world, from dying from ailments. If any part of the body dies, your whole body shuts down, symbolizing humanity's fall in the event a part of the collapses. We are one body, all equal.

  1. Click Play.
  2. If you have enough heals, click injured areas of the body.
  3. Injury levels: White(Healthiest) - Green - Yellow - Red(Most Critical)
  4. Heals will regenerate over time.
  5. Prevent any part from reaching critical level and dying.
  6. Save Humanity!
